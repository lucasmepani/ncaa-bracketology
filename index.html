NCAA Bracketology

Tournament Projection Tool

Purpose

This tool projects what the NCAA Tournament bracket will look like based on game outcomes you select. See how different results affect tournament seeding, at-large bids, and regional matchups.

How It Works

Select Game Winners: Click on upcoming games to pick winners and see real-time bracket updates.

View Options: Filter games by date or by team to find specific matchups you want to project.

Visualize Bracket: Click the "Visualize Bracket" button to see the complete tournament bracket with seeding, play-in games, and regional assignments.
